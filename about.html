A little about me...

My name is Lidiya Hosana. I'm 17, a Grade 11 student in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I've always been curious about how things work – especially in biology and genetics. In early 2025, I started learning HTML and CSS in my Web Design class. I could create pages with headings, images, and even tables! Now I'm building this portfolio to share my journey.

I believe that code and science both help us understand the world – and one day I hope to combine them as a surgeon, doctor, or biochemist.
